How it works

The embedded stream shows a view of my balcony, which is festively decorated with Christmas lights that you will be controlling. The balcony faces El Camino Real, a busy road, so give everybody a good show.

  • Click the buttons above or use your keyboard to toggle the strands on and off.
  • If other users are playing with the lights, you can see their changes in real time. Don't let that stop you from joining in.
  • If nobody touches the lights for more than thirty seconds, they start doing their own thing. That's your cue to come up with something more creative than what I've programmed.
  • Each strand is rate limited, so you can't strobe any one strand super fast. Let's not give anybody a seizure. (The rate limit is enforced on the server, but nice try.)

The relays array are controlled by a Raspberry Pi.
